What we offer
Everything your plants are trying to tell you.
🌱

Soil Intelligence

Real-time readings on moisture, pH, nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium — translated into plain language advice you can act on today.

📡

Wireless Sensors

Our thumb-sized sensors connect over Bluetooth and last 18 months on a single charge. Stick one in any pot or raised bed in seconds.

🔔

Smart Alerts

Get notified before problems become disasters. Verdana warns you about upcoming frosts, drought stress, and early pest signals.

📅

Grow Calendar

A personalised planting calendar built from your local climate, your soil, and the specific varieties you're growing this season.

🤖

AI Diagnosis

Snap a photo of a wilting leaf or discoloured stem. Our model cross-references 800+ diseases and deficiencies and tells you exactly what's wrong.

🌍

Community Insights

See how other gardeners in your microclimate are doing. Crowdsourced tips surfaced automatically to your dashboard when they're relevant.
